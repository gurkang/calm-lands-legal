Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Calm Lands Companion ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

The app collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Creature capture counts for each playthrough
  • Playthrough names and creation dates
  • Active playthrough selection

This data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Track your creature capture progress
  • Manage multiple playthroughs
  • Display statistics and completion status

Your data remains on your device and is never accessed, analyzed, or shared by us.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses RevenueCat for in-app purchase processing. When you make a purchase:

  • RevenueCat processes your transaction through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data for fraud prevention
  • We do not receive or store your payment information

For more information about RevenueCat's privacy practices, please visit: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

All game progress data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. The app does not:

  • Upload your data to any server
  • Create backups in the cloud
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your usage or analytics

Your data is as secure as your device. We recommend using device-level security features like passcodes or biometric authentication.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed. To delete all data:

  • Uninstall the app from your device
  • Or use the "Delete Playthrough" feature within the app to remove specific playthroughs

Deleting the app will permanently remove all creature capture data and playthroughs from your device. This action cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, there is no collection of personal information from users of any age.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: Your data is always accessible within the app
  • Modification: You can modify capture counts and playthrough names at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete playthroughs or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Portability: Your data never leaves your device, so there is no data to export

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
